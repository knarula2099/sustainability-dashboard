Key Insights

  • Top barriers to sustainable practices are cost concerns and perception that attendees don't care
  • 73% of attendees would pay more for sustainable events, primarily in the 5-10% range
  • Attendee demand is the top motivator for event hosts to prioritize sustainability
  • Current sustainable practices are limited, with food donation being the most common
  • Event hosts need better resources and guidelines to implement sustainable practices
